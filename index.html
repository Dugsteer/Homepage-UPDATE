Welcome to Esl-ology.com

Here you will find free tools for English lessons that can be used online and in the classroom. There are flashcards, memory games, hangman, grammar mazes and much more, all designed to help your classes. More material will be added regularly, so please check back to see what's new! With very best wishes from Esl-ology.

Frogification

A Halloween observation game. Students have to say which characters have been turned into frogs. It starts hard but gets easier. Check out the how-to video on YouTube.

Grammar Mazes

All of the (upcoming) grammar mazes help student awareness of grammar mistakes. The aim is to have a clean run all the way through. Another way of looking at it is 'stealth drilling'.

Vocabulary Bingo Cards

Use a computer to generate and print out random sets of bingo cards for a quick, fun way to back up learning vocabulary sets. Use the online caller to see which comes next.

Flash Cards

A great way to present or revise vocabulary sets with a class, either with traditional flashcards or modern 'hover cards', these are available both with images and with photos.

Talk for a Minute

Do you have something to say? Could you talk for a minute about it in class, without hesitation, repetition, bad English or going off topic? It's time to find out!

HALLOWEEN HANGMAN

Hangman is always useful as a warmer or to fill the last five minutes. Now it's even easier with our online version. Choose a word or let the website do it for you. Have fun!
